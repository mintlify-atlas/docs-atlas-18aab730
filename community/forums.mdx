## Google Groups Forum

The [iDempiere Google Groups forum](https://groups.google.com/group/idempiere) is the main platform for:

- **Questions and support** - Get help from experienced community members
- **Feature discussions** - Propose and discuss new ideas before creating JIRA tickets
- **Announcements** - Stay updated on releases, events, and important news
- **Best practices** - Learn from implementation experiences and success stories
- **Problem solving** - Work through complex issues with community expertise

### Getting Started with the Forum

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Group">
    Visit [groups.google.com/group/idempiere](https://groups.google.com/group/idempiere) and click "Join group" to become a member.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search Before Posting">
    Use the search function to check if your question has already been answered. Many common topics have existing discussions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Clear Topics">
    When posting:
    - Use a descriptive subject line
    - Provide context and relevant details
    - Include version information
    - Attach screenshots if helpful
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Engage with Responses">
    - Respond to follow-up questions
    - Mark helpful answers
    - Share your solution if you find one
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Forum Best Practices

<Check>Use clear, descriptive subject lines that summarize your question</Check>
<Check>Include relevant system information (iDempiere version, database, OS)</Check>
<Check>Provide reproduction steps for issues</Check>
<Check>Search for existing discussions before posting</Check>

## Mattermost Chat

The [iDempiere Mattermost server](https://mattermost.idempiere.org) provides real-time communication channels for the community.

### Available Channels

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Support Channel" icon="life-ring">
    The [support channel](https://mattermost.idempiere.org/idempiere/channels/support) is active during business hours and provides:
    - Quick answers to common questions
    - Real-time troubleshooting assistance
    - Community interaction and networking
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Development Channels" icon="code">
    Connect with developers working on iDempiere core and plugins:
    - Discuss code contributions
    - Coordinate development efforts
    - Share technical insights
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Topic-Specific Channels" icon="hashtag">
    Find channels dedicated to specific topics:
    - Implementation discussions
    - Regional/language-specific groups
    - Special interest topics
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### When to Use Mattermost

Mattermost is ideal for:

- **Quick questions** that need fast answers
- **Real-time collaboration** on issues or features
- **Informal discussions** and community networking
- **Coordinating** development or testing efforts
- **Getting feedback** before formal proposals

<Note>
  For detailed technical discussions or questions that require async responses, the Google Groups forum is often more appropriate. Mattermost messages can be ephemeral, while forum posts are permanently searchable.
</Note>

## Discussion Guidelines

### Code of Conduct

All community interactions are governed by the [iDempiere Code of Conduct](https://github.com/idempiere/idempiere/blob/master/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Key principles:

- **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with courtesy and professionalism
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome participants from all backgrounds and experience levels
- **Be collaborative** - Work together constructively
- **Be patient** - Remember this is a volunteer community

### Effective Communication

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Asking Questions">
    When seeking help:
    
    1. **Provide context** - Explain what you're trying to accomplish
    2. **Include details** - Version, environment, specific error messages
    3. **Show what you've tried** - Demonstrate your troubleshooting efforts
    4. **Be specific** - Clear, focused questions get better answers
    5. **Format code and logs** - Use code blocks for readability
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Proposing Ideas">
    When suggesting enhancements:
    
    1. **Explain the problem** - What current limitation are you addressing?
    2. **Describe your solution** - How would your proposal work?
    3. **Justify the value** - Why would this benefit the community?
    4. **Consider alternatives** - Could this be a plugin instead of core?
    5. **Gather feedback** - Be open to discussion and refinement
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Helping Others">
    When providing assistance:
    
    1. **Be patient** - Not everyone has the same expertise level
    2. **Explain clearly** - Provide step-by-step guidance when needed
    3. **Share knowledge** - Point to relevant documentation
    4. **Stay constructive** - Focus on solutions, not criticism
    5. **Follow up** - Check if your suggestion resolved the issue
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Topic Organization

### When to Use Each Platform

| Platform | Best For | Response Time |
|----------|----------|---------------|
| **Google Groups** | Detailed questions, feature discussions, permanent reference | Hours to days |
| **Mattermost** | Quick questions, real-time collaboration, informal chat | Minutes (during business hours) |
| **JIRA** | Confirmed bugs, approved feature requests, tracking issues | Varies by priority |
| **Wiki** | Documentation, guides, permanent reference material | Self-service |

### Escalation Path

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with Discussion">
    Begin in forums or Mattermost to discuss your question, idea, or potential bug.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Gather Community Feedback">
    Collect input and confirmation from community members. Refine your understanding of the issue.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create JIRA Ticket">
    Once confirmed and discussed, create a formal ticket in [JIRA](https://idempiere.atlassian.net) for bugs or approved enhancements.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Progress">
    Follow the JIRA ticket for updates and implementation progress.
  </Step>
</Steps>
