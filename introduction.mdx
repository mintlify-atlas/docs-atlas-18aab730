iDempiere is a powerful, enterprise-grade **ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)**, **CRM (Customer Relationship Management)**, and **SCM (Supply Chain Management)** solution built by the community, for the community.

<Note>
  iDempiere evolved from ADempiere and Compiere, carrying decades of refined business logic and real-world enterprise experience.
</Note>

## Core Capabilities

iDempiere provides comprehensive functionality to manage your entire business:

### Financial Management

- **General Ledger** - Complete accounting with multi-currency support
- **Accounts Payable & Receivable** - Automated invoice processing and payment tracking
- **Asset Management** - Track and depreciate fixed assets
- **Banking** - Reconciliation, payments, and cash management

### Sales & CRM

- **Order Management** - From quotation to delivery
- **Customer Relationship Management** - Track interactions, opportunities, and campaigns
- **Pricing & Discounts** - Flexible pricing schemes and promotional rules
- **Commission Tracking** - Automated sales commission calculations

### Purchasing & Inventory

- **Purchase Orders** - Streamlined procurement workflows
- **Inventory Management** - Real-time stock tracking across multiple warehouses
- **Material Requirements Planning** - Automated replenishment
- **Product Management** - Complete product catalog with attributes and BOMs

### Manufacturing & Distribution

- **Bill of Materials** - Multi-level BOM management
- **Production Planning** - Work orders and resource scheduling
- **Warehouse Management** - Advanced inventory operations
- **Shipping & Receiving** - Complete logistics tracking

## Technology Stack

iDempiere leverages modern, proven technologies:

- **Backend**: Java with OSGi (Eclipse RCP)
- **Web UI**: ZK Framework for rich web applications
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (recommended) or Oracle
- **Application Server**: Embedded Jetty
- **Reporting**: JasperReports
- **API**: REST and SOAP web services
